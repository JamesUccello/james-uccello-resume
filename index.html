About Me

My life has been full of great challenges and rewards. I'm a proud father of 2 wonderful kids. A Journeyman Wireman Electrician and now a Fullstack Web Developer. During my time as a Wireman Electrician, Ive been able to travel, make new friends, and be part of a team building some great projects such as:

Facebook MPK22

Apple Infinite Campus

Google Campus

The time I spent on the road building these projects has allowed me to grow personally by learning strong work skills needed to function as a team member instead of as a individual. I learned many skills along the way that have taken me from a fresh apprentice level Wireman all the way up to a Journeyman Wiremen in a leadership role.

Being part of the team working with great individuals, put me on the path to the Motion Picture Industry. I passed the necessary classes, obtained my Class A license, and with the help of my new friends, I joined the Motion Picture Industry as a Motion Picture Electrician on set.

Unfortunately times changed, the Motion Picture industry slowed down forcing me to pivot once more.

Inspired by my kids to try new things, even when these things might be a little scary. I sat before this computer screen elevating myself, by taking on a new challenge of becoming a Full Stack Developer. I cant wait to see what the future holds and how all these trials will intertwine with each other.

If you want to know about me and the person I am, just look back at this page and think to yourself, "is this a person who has the ability to learn, adjust and move forward as a strong coder? Or will he fold when things get tough?" You bet your ass I will Succeed! I will fight through the challenges, continue to learn, adapt, and move forward to be the best Web developer I can be, in order to achieve 1 purpose "The stability of my families future!"

Success in life is not achieved by not taking chances and not facing our fears, but by how we push through new obstacles in our path to reach the other side.
